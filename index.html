🎯 How It Works

🎨 The pixel color randomization by an algorithm, not pre-made GIFs.

🚀 Each time, a new color palette is randomly selected and applied to the image, creating a unique look 🌈.

With multiple palettes containing 5 harmonious colors, the chance of repeating the exact same pattern is approximately 1 in 10×5^1024 (if there are 10 palettes)—an extraordinarily large number, far beyond easy comprehension 😱. It’s so vast that it’s practically impossible to find the same combination in our lifetime! 🤯
